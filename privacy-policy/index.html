Privacy Policy

Last updated: August 14, 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how Machina Coach ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares personal data when you use our services, including when you communicate with us via WhatsApp and other messaging channels (collectively, the "Services").

By using the Services, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

  • Account and Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and any profile details you provide.

  • Payment/Subscription Data: Limited billing details processed by our payment processor. We do not store full payment card numbers.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide, operate, and improve the Services, including coaching interactions and support.
  • To respond to your messages (including via WhatsApp) and provide customer support.
  • To personalize your experience and deliver relevant content.
  • To monitor usage, troubleshoot, and maintain security of the Services.
  • To comply with legal obligations and enforce our terms.

3. Legal Bases for Processing

If you are in the EEA/UK, we process your data under the following bases:

  • Contractual necessity (to deliver the Services you request).
  • Legitimate interests (e.g., improving Services, ensuring security).
  • Consent (e.g., for certain communications or analytics where required). You can withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal obligation (where we must comply with applicable laws).

4. Sharing of Information

  • Service Providers: We use trusted vendors to help operate our Services (e.g., hosting, analytics, communications, payment processing). They are bound by confidentiality and data protection obligations.

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary to provide the Services, comply with our legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce agreements. Retention periods may vary by data type and context (e.g., customer support logs vs. billing records).

6. Security

We implement technical and organizational measures designed to protect your information. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure; we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights such as access, correction, deletion, portability, restriction/objection to processing, and the right to withdraw consent. To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.
